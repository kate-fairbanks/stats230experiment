Welcome to the Reading Comprehension Quiz

You will be shown a short passage to read. After reading, you will take a 5-question quiz based on what you remember. You will have 30 seconds to read the passage, and 60 seconds to answer the questions. You will not be able to view the passage again during the quiz, so read carefully.

1. What kind of tree is described in the passage?





2. What do squirrels do around the tree?





3. Where do birds nest in the tree?





4. What do children often do near the tree?





5. How is the oak tree described in the last sentence?
